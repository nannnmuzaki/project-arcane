Season 1 of Arcane explores the origins of Piltover and Zaun’s enduring conflict. Piltover, the “City of Progress,” thrives on innovation, while Zaun, its undercity, suffers from poverty and oppression. The discovery of Hextech, a technology merging magic and science, promises great progress but also fuels greed and unrest. Amid this turmoil, sisters Vi and Jinx are torn apart by tragedy, setting them on opposing paths. As political tensions and personal vendettas collide, the fragile balance between these two cities spirals toward chaos.

Zaun is a city across the river from Piltover, a large portion of which runs beneath the latter. Whereas its upper counterpart is thriving and rich, Zaun is the opposite, becoming almost literally the catch basin of the waste from the city above it where its residents try to survive from scraps spared to them. One known part of Zaun is the Lanes.

Piltover is a renowned progressive city, dubbed the City of Progress for its innovations and Academy. The city became particularly famous for its Hexgates, the scientific utilization of what was dubbed  Hextech  by its  Piltovan pioneer , which revolutionized travel and trade for the rest of the world, for which they have since become the global shipping lane.

INTRODUCTION

Arcane tells the story of Piltover and Zaun, two cities divided by wealth and ideals. As Piltover thrives on innovation, Zaun struggles in chaos. The series follows characters like Vi and Jinx, exploring themes of family, betrayal, and ambition. The discovery of Hextech technology sparks conflict, threatening the fragile balance between the cities.
